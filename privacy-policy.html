Privacy Policy for QR Scanner & Generator

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

QR Scanner & Generator ("we", "our", "us") may collect the following types of information:

  • Camera Permission: Used only for scanning QR codes and barcodes.
  • Storage Permission: Used to save generated QR codes locally on your device.
  • Device and Usage Information: Such as device model, operating system, crash logs, and app performance data collected through Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Used by Google AdMob to display advertisements and measure ad performance.

2. User-Provided QR Code Data

The QR Code Generator allows users to optionally enter information such as:

  • Text content
  • Website URLs
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Contact details
  • Wi-Fi credentials

This information is processed locally on the user's device only for generating QR codes. It is processed temporarily in memory (ephemerally) and is not stored, transmitted, or shared with our servers or any third parties.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide QR code scanning and generation functionality
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Display advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Enable in-app purchases via Google Play Billing
  • Analyze usage trends and diagnose crashes

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that may collect information according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob – Advertising services
  • Google Firebase – Analytics, crash reporting, performance monitoring
  • Google Play Billing – In-app purchases

Learn more:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage

Scan history and generated QR codes are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this information to our servers.

6. Advertising and Personalization

Advertisements displayed in the app may be personalized based on advertising identifiers. Users can reset or disable ad personalization through their device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Your Rights

  • Clear scan history anytime within the app
  • Control advertising preferences via device settings
  • Uninstall the app at any time to stop data collection

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
