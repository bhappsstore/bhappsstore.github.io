Privacy Policy for QR Code Scanner

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

QR Code Scanner ("we", "our", "us") collects the following information:

  • Camera Permission: Used only for scanning QR codes
  • Storage Permission: Used to save generated QR codes
  • Device Information: For analytics and crash reporting
  • Ad Identifiers: For serving relevant advertisements

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide QR code scanning and generation services
  • To display personalized advertisements
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To maintain scan history locally on your device

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Billing: For in-app purchases (Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase: For analytics and remote configuration (Privacy Policy)

4. Data Storage

All scan history and user preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not store or transmit your scan data to our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your scan history anytime from the app settings
  • Disable data collection for advertisements (via device settings)
  • Request deletion of your data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
