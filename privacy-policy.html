Privacy Policy for QR Scanner & Generator

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

QR Scanner & Generator ("we", "our", "us") collects the following information:

  • Camera Permission: Used only for scanning QR codes and barcodes.
  • Storage Permission: Used to save generated QR codes locally on your device.
  • Device Information: For analytics, performance monitoring, and crash reporting.
  • Advertising Identifiers: For displaying relevant advertisements via AdMob.

2. QR Code Generator

The app includes a QR Code Generator that allows users to create QR codes using:

  • Text
  • Website URLs
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Wi-Fi credentials
  • Other supported input types

All generated QR codes are processed locally on your device. We do not store or transmit this data to any external server.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide QR code scanning and generation services
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To display personalized advertisements
  • To enable in-app purchases
  • To analyze app usage and fix technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services, which may collect information according to their privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: For ads. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: For in-app purchases. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google LLC): For analytics, crash reporting, and remote configuration. Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage

All scan history and generated QR code data are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to any external servers.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no digital method of storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

  • Clear your scan history anytime from app settings
  • Disable data collection for advertisements via device settings
  • Uninstall the app at any time to stop data collection
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
